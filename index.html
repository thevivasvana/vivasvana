About The Vivasvana

Vivasvana is a plant-based nutrition company. We make clean, natural protein powders from a thoughtful mix of millets, grains, pulses, spices, and other nature-based ingredients. Our goal is simple: give your body honest nutrition while caring for nature and the people who grow with us.

Nature knows balance. At The Vivasvana, we follow that balance—nourishing your day with plant power, supporting our farmers and artisans, and protecting the earth that feeds us all.

Natural · Vegan · Gluten-Free

Meet The Vivasvana Plant-Based Protein—a simple, trustworthy way to add protein to your day. We start with millets, known for being hardy and naturally nutritious, then balance them with grains and pulses for a fuller amino-acid profile. A small touch of traditional spices adds taste and everyday comfort. Every batch is made small, by trained village producers who follow strict hygiene and quality checks for taste, texture, and consistency.

Stir one scoop into water, milk, or plant milk for a quick drink, or fold into smoothies, oats, rotis, dosa/pancakes, or energy balls. Designed with care for people, nature, and local livelihoods, this is plant protein you can feel good about using—clean, familiar, and easy to understand.

Vision

To nourish everyday life—advancing human health, and a regenerative planet through trusted plant-based nutrition.

Mission

  1. • To offer natural, preservative-free nutrition powders, formulated with both traditional wisdom and modern nutritional science.
  2. • To empower village-based producer groups, promoting inclusive growth through fair, hygienic, and small-batch manufacturing.
  3. • To uphold the highest standards of clean-label transparency, ethical sourcing, and community responsibility in every aspect of the business.

Brand Values

  • Honesty: Clear ingredients, clear methods.
  • Care: For your health, our teams, and the planet.
  • Respect: For traditional foods and local knowledge.
  • Learning: We listen, test, and improve.
  • Stewardship: We leave things better than we found them.
